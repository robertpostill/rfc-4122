 (require rfc-4122) package: rfc-4122-lib

rfc-4122 generates ids. Speicifically UUIDs that adhere to the RFC-4122 standard.

If you haven’t had the warnings about UUIDs let me spell them out for you now:

  • They’re not private, especially the ones based on MAC addresses

  • They’re not easy to validate. In that I can tell you it looks like a UUID, but is it a fake? I can’t easlily define that.

  • You can generate collisions, especially if you are a bad actor
